How to Convert Glucose mg/dL to mmol/L

There are several ways to convert glucose measurements from milligrams per deciliter (mg/dL) to millimoles per liter (mmol/L):

1. Use the Conversion Formula

The formula for converting mg/dL to mmol/L is:

mmol/L = mg/dL รท 18

For example, to convert 150 mg/dL to mmol/L:

mmol/L = 150 mg/dL รท 18 = 8.33 mmol/L
